ABOUT US

QUANTUN

Engineering DPS

Quantun Engineering DPS is a consumer electronic manufacturing company that specialized in production of Bulbs, Solar Panels, Solar Streetlights, Floodlights, Solar Generators and other electrical and electronic devices and services. This is just the starting products which will be expanded to hundreds within the first year. Samples of our products have undergone serious and rigorous tests by Standard Organization of Nigeria(SON) and had the of relevant product certifications (MANCAP)
  • It is an indigenous company founded and sited in Bauchi, by Bauchi State Citizens to help in providing jobs and for our teeming unemployed youths as well as to improving the internally generated revenue (LGR) of the country in General
  • The country is a multi disciplinary engineering setup with different cadres and engineering skills as contemporarily, the society is becoming increasingly global and technologically advanced. It is obvious that the country is becoming more populous with high level of unemployed youths. As an engineering and manufacturing company, we have the capacity to provide very good trainings to these youths to improve their employability, increase their confidence and skills to make themselves self-reliant as prerequisite for sustainable peace to prevailed
